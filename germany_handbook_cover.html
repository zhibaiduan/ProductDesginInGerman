Why this exists
Legal teams explain what's prohibited. Not what to build.
Most compliance resources tell you what you can't do. This handbook tells you what to do instead — and shows you the cases where it worked commercially.
The grey zones move faster than any guide can keep up.
GTM consent rulings, SCHUFA AI decisions, DPF instability — the orange lines from 2024 are different from the orange lines of 2025. We track what's actually changing.
The green lines are a genuine competitive advantage — if you know them.
Nextcloud tripled its pipeline in six months. Personio cut procurement cycles from 3 months to 3 weeks. These were product and positioning decisions, not just legal ones.
Reading is one thing. Knowing how you'd actually decide is another.
The quiz at the end tests judgment, not memory. Most people discover at least one thing they thought was safe that isn't — before they find out the hard way.
Who this is not for
You might not need this if...
You already have a German data protection lawyer on retainer reviewing every product decision. (Lucky you.)
Your product has zero EU users and no plans to enter the European market in the next 3 years.
You're looking for a legal opinion you can cite in court. This is a product builder's map, not a legal brief.
You only work in B2C consumer apps with no enterprise or regulated-industry ambitions.
A note on legal advice
This handbook is research-based orientation material for product teams. Every situation is different. Before making high-stakes compliance decisions, talk to a qualified German data protection lawyer. Think of this as the briefing before that conversation — not the substitute for it.
